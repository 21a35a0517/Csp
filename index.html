FOOD WASTE MANAGEMENT

This Web portal represents crowd resourcing on food waste management, where the leftover food is donated to the needy and to people who lack or suffer from food. The need for donations is due to the wastage of food. In this present scenario, food wastage is a large quantity in many places, like restaurants, weddings, social functions, college canteens, and many other social events. Some people donate food manually by visiting some organizations. In order to reduce the issue of food wastage, there are Web sites that have made efforts to help people donate food through online mode. This system provides a new approach where leftover food is donated to needy people or organizations, benefiting both governmental and non-governmental people. The system is an effective means of donating food to organizations, orphanages, etc., over the Internet. A new donation request will be created on the portal, and once the request is accepted, the food request from food donor to food receiver will be processed and updated on the webpage.

Donating "FOOD" is not just about giving the leftover food to someone else, but also having a Superior Intension of saving "ANOTHER LIFE"

We are from the CSE department. We conducted a survey recently and we found a common problem: most of the leftover food from restaurants and function halls is dumped. So we thought of donating the leftover food from restaurants and function halls to the nearest orphanages to ensure that the leftover food does not go to waste.
